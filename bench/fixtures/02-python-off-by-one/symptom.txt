get_page(items=list(range(10)), page=2, per_page=5) returns [] silently — but page 2 should be out of range (pages 0 and 1 already cover all 10 items). The boundary check is meant to reject out-of-range pages but lets page 2 through. get_page(items, 3, 5) DOES raise ValueError("page 3 out of range (max 2)") — so the check fires for some out-of-range pages, just one off.
